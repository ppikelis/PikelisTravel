#Visited #Every #Country #In #Europe! ✅
Feels incredible — but also a little scary... because all the "nearby & easy" countries are now done. 😅 #MaliAnyone? 😂
Romania was country #140! #TimeFliesCountryNumbersAlso 🚀
#Romania was so chill and picturesque: cozy little towns with cute cafés, local restaurants (with delicious but heavy local dishes), historic castles, hundreds of churches (seriously, why so many? 🤔), and very welcoming hosts.
While most of the country is pretty flat, the Carpathian Mountains deliver some awesome hikes and drives — especially the #Transfăgărășan road. 👌
We were also promised bears... but (un)fortunately, they never showed up.
Bucharest surprised us too — a lively city with a strong flavor of the communist past, especially the gargantuan Parliament building (the heaviest building in the world — hurry up, it's sinking a little every year! 😂).
