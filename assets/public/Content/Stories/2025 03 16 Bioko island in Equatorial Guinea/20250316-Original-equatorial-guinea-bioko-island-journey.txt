#Day8. #EquatorialGuinea! 🇬🇶
This concludes the quest to visit all 'Guineas' in Africa ✅ 😂 frankly, it felt more like being in one of Spain's oversea territories rather than in a standard African country. The country has significant oil reserves which allows the Vice President (the son of the President, of course) to own an impressive collection of 30 luxury cars, including 2 Bugatti Veyrons and 1 Koenigsegg. Which makes sense because Bioko island is small, so quick acceleration is key! 🤣
The country opened up to tourists only recently, so it's still complicated to roam around - e.g., one needs military permits and, of course, a blessing from a local chief to visit a waterfall 🤧 at least no permits for chilling with locals on the beach ;))
