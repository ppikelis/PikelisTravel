#Tuvalu #Funafuti #WhereIsThat?
Warm welcome from the second least visited country in the world! 🗺 (Beating Nauru by mightly 1.5k visitors!). Talking about the firsts, I am pretty sure that we are the first honeymooners here! 👌
A few fun facts?
- A third of the width of island is a runway that caters for 2-3 flights every week and mostly serves as a gathering place for locals in the evenings. How about a ferry? The nearest country is only 3 days away by boat.. 😅
- Locals are very friendly, peaceful and calm. There's no rush - it's mighty 13km to walk the country end to end 💪 Though, locals prefer motorbikes over walking.. combined with eating loads of processed food (there's little fresh food on the island) is a recipe for obesity. #SimilarToUSA
- Tuvalans are very religious - especially when it comes to not working on Sundays. It took us quite some effort to find someone to take us on a boat ride 😂 on the bright side, we were the only ones visiting the atols nearby and had sandy beaches, palm trees and crystal clear waters all to ourselves 🏝 👩‍❤️‍👨
- Nearly got a fine from police for... merely walking on the road between 18:45 and 19:00. It's devotion time & everyone must stop any activities they do🙏 If you ask me, it's a quite cute rule 🙌
