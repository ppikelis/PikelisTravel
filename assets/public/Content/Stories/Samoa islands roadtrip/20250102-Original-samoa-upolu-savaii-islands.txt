#Talofa from Samoa! 🇼🇸

Pristine beaches, waterfalls, turtles and very laid back people – that's what we came here for! 🥥🏝🐢

Samoans don't have much, but never skip an opportunity to show off their great talent in painting, coloring & crafts 😅 it gives quite a charm when you drive around and see all the colorful flowers, flower pots, fences, houses - feels like a decoration competition and each community wants to impress! 🥇🥈🥉 If that's not enough, then add thousands of roadside flags and Christmas lights (which they love!) to the mix.. #DizzyColorful 🙈

Though, what surprised us the most was the number of churches! #VeryReligious! Felt like each tiny village has at least one church! #HugeDensity! #SeeLastPicture! 😳
