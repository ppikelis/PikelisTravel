#Day0. #MountCameroon.
Me and Linas arrived in Cameroon one day early to tick off one checkpoint we missed last time – climbing the highest peak in Cameroon 🇨🇲 🙄 #4085m

The flight was delayed, our luggage didn’t arrive, traffic was a mess… that left us with 2 hours of sleep before we found ourselves at the base of the mountain 😭

Normally, this hike takes two days… sounds reasonable knowing that it's barely 3km of ascent and 25km of trekking 😂 But since we’re in full rally mode, we decided to go for it in one day! 😉 there's a yearly race up the mountain and the winners finish it in 4-5 hours 🤔 plus we have a guide - so we are all set! 👌

In the end we reached the summit in 7.5 hours 💪 We lost our original guide early on – he was too slow 😅 But we found another one on the way – wearing rubber boots! 😂

The path is just straight up - no switchbacks. It was so steep at times we had to climb on all fours 😳 We hoped to run back down in a couple of hours… But then it was drizzling the whole day and the rocks got slippery. It took us another 6 hours and multiple slips to get down 🙋 interestingly, the last hour in the jungle in the dark - we could hear animals all over the place #NoLions #NoElephants but still not comfortable with jungle sounds.. 😅
